About Us

Welcome to our blog dedicated to new parents and the beautiful journey of welcoming a newborn into the world. As parents ourselves, we understand the joys and challenges that come with this life-changing experience.

Our mission is to provide valuable resources, support, and insights to help new parents navigate the early days of parenthood. We cover a wide range of topics, including newborn care, parenting tips, health and wellness, and emotional support.

Why We Started This Blog

Becoming a parent can be overwhelming. We started this blog to create a community where parents can find reliable information, share their experiences, and connect with others who are on the same journey. We believe that every parent deserves support and encouragement.

What You Can Expect

On our blog, you can expect:

  • Informative articles on newborn care and parenting
  • Tips for managing the challenges of new parenthood
  • Personal stories and experiences from our parenting journey
  • Resources and recommendations for products and services

We invite you to join us on this incredible journey of parenthood. Together, we can learn, grow, and celebrate the joys of raising our little ones!

About Peekaboo

Becoming a mom introduced a new dimension to my identity, transforming my perception of ambition and success. At 29, I had already achieved my dream role through dedication, hard work, and unrelenting strides beyond my comfort zone. But with the arrival of my first child, the dream job I had so passionately chased suddenly lost its luster. I was caught in the dichotomy of wanting to be present as a mother and seeking fulfillment in a thrilling career. I felt like I couldn’t lean into either side without sacrificing something. So I spun up a passion project, now turned full time career.

A resource to help you simplify the daily chaos of motherhood. But Peekaboo is more than just a publishing platform, it’s a mission to empower mothers striving to find that delicate balance between a rewarding career and cherishing precious moments of motherhood. It's about living a fulfilled, balanced, present, and beautiful life amidst all the chaos.
